Now, let's get on with the
one-time setup!
This app also includes a
widget. For widget to work, it
must be added after the barcode has been set. So, if you've already added it, please remove the
widget and add it after reading
the instructions.
Instructions on how to
setup:
Start by noting down your card number. It looks something like
the following:
608123 45678 9012 3456
and is found under the
barcode in the app
Go back from this page
and into the page. There,
enter the numbers. Once done, press to save. Now add the widget for easy access to
barcode. Or you can also press
in the app to see
barcode.
